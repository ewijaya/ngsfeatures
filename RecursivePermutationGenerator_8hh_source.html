7 * Description: Class for computing permutation. Does the real work
8 * but is called through PermutationGenerator.
9 *
10 * works on the following recursive description of a permutation generator.
11 *
12 * for( i = 0; i <= n; ++i ){
13 * 1. place n in position i
14 * 2. generator all permutations of 0..n-1 in the other positions
15 * }
16 *
17 * Caveat: This is a home brewed algorithm and I suspect it is
18 * a bit slow. I am sure that somewhere, perhaps
19 * Knuth's book on permutations one may find a better one.
20 *
21 * Purpose: Originally created to compute exact p-values of the
22 * rank correlation statistic for small sample sizes
23 * for which approximations may be poor.
24 */
